# Soul Imaging: Billing & Appointments (Bulk Billing)

## Appointment Scheduling
Soul Imaging offers high flexibility for patients needing urgent diagnostic attention.
* We provide **same-day or next-day appointments** subject to availability.
* To book an appointment, patients must provide their full Name, the specific Type of Scan needed (e.g., Ultrasound, X-Ray), and a Preferred Appointment Day. 
* All bookings must include an active medical referral from a Doctor or General Practitioner.

## Medicare & Bulk Billing
* **We Accept All Referrals:** Even if your referral slip has another radiology clinic's name on it, you can legally bring it to Soul Imaging, and we will honor it.
* **100% Bulk Billing:** The majority of standard X-Rays, CT Scans, and Medicare-eligible Ultrasounds are completely Bulk Billed (Zero out-of-pocket costs) for Australian permanent residents equipped with a valid Medicare card. There are a few specialized scans that may incur a minor gap fee—our receptionists will notify you explicitly if this happens.
