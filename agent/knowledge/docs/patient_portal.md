# Soul Imaging: Patient Portal and Scan Results

We understand that awaiting diagnostic results is stressful. We securely store and deliver all X-Ray, CT, and Ultrasound results digitally through our advanced Patient Portal System.

## Accessing Your Images
Once your scan is finalized by our certified radiologist, a unique SMS message will be sent directly to your registered mobile phone.
1. The SMS will contain a secure activation link.
2. Clicking this link directs you to the Soul Imaging Patient Web App (available on iOS, Android, and Web browsers).
3. The App will securely display both your finalized Radiologist Report as well as the high-resolution diagnostic images.

## Report Delivery
With the automated Patient Portal, there's no need to return to the clinic to retrieve physical film or a CD unless strictly requested by your referring medical specialist. Your Doctor receives a secure electronic copy of your results at the exact same time you do via the app!
